BISMONTAG PEACE

Analysis · De-Escalation · Transparency

Mission

BISMONTAG PEACE is an independent project focused on the analysis of global conflicts. The project aims to provide structured, transparent and accessible information about wars, political crises and geopolitical tensions around the world.

By combining analysis, mapping and documentation, the project seeks to improve the understanding of conflict dynamics and possible paths toward de-escalation.

Conflict Atlas

The BISMONTAG Conflict Atlas documents global conflicts since 1945. It connects geographic information, historical timelines and analytical frameworks to create a structured overview of international conflict dynamics.

The atlas is designed as a continuously evolving public resource.

Principles

Analysis before alignment.

The project focuses on understanding conflicts rather than supporting specific actors. Careful analysis should precede political positioning.

Transparency.

Sources, assumptions and uncertainties are documented whenever possible.

Accessibility.

Complex geopolitical dynamics should be understandable for a broad audience.
